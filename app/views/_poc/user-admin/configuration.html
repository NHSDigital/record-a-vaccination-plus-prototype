Help with these options

ICB (Integrated Care Board)

Integrated Care Boards (ICBs) are NHS organisations that oversee local health services by planning care, managing budgets, and working with partners to support community health needs.

LSOA (Lower Layer Super Output Area)

Lower Layer Super Output Areas (LSOAs) are small geographic units made up of 4 or 5 Output Areas (OAs), typically containing 400 to 1,200 households and 1,000 to 3,000 residents.

Location

Location is where you live, work, or physical geographical location.
