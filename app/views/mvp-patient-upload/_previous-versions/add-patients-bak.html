How to prepare and upload a CSV file

Preparing a CSV file

First, export patient information from the system that you use to store and manage patient records. You must export the information as a CSV file.

For each patient, include their:

  • name
  • NHS number
  • pregnancy due date

Do not edit the CSV file before you upload it.

It is your responsibility to make sure the patient information is accurate.

Uploading a CSV file

When you upload a CSV file, we check to see what vaccination information we currently hold about your patients.

Information:

It may take up to 30 minutes to carry out this check.

You will not be able to view your existing list of patients during this time.

You will receive an email when the check is complete.

If the upload is successful, you will be able to view a list of all your patients that show their personal details alongside their vaccination history.
