Upload new patient cohort list

Use this function to upload new and updated patient cohort lists to RAVS. It is important to upload all patients currently in the care of your department. You can remove patients from this cohort list.

Guidance on how to structure inputted data

Data required:

  • All patients where: pregnancy status = yes AND week 10+ into pregnancy
  • Patient name
  • NHS number
  • Pregnancy status = yes
  • Confirmation that each vaccination has been explained and offered
  • Due date
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Contact preferences
